Design and build a complete, modern, multi-page website for **Wise Farmer** 
(wise-farmer.com) — an agri-tech education and empowerment platform that 
equips farmers with sustainable practices, cutting-edge agricultural 
technologies, online courses, global market access, webinars, scholarships, 
and expert consulting. The brand serves smallholder and commercial farmers 
across Africa and beyond, bridging traditional farming wisdom with modern 
innovation.

---

## 🎨 BRAND IDENTITY & DESIGN SYSTEM

**Brand Personality:** Trustworthy, forward-thinking, rooted, empowering, 
scientific yet approachable.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary Green: #2D6A4F (deep forest green — growth, trust)
- Accent Green: #52B788 (fresh mid-green — innovation, vitality)
- Earth Gold: #D4A017 (warm harvest gold — prosperity, warmth)
- Soil Brown: #6B4226 (deep earth — roots, heritage)
- Off-White: #F4F9F4 (clean background — clarity)
- Dark Charcoal: #1A1A1A (primary text)
- Light Sage: #B7E4C7 (subtle highlights, cards)

**Typography:**
- Headings: "Fraunces" or "Playfair Display" — editorial, organic, bold
- Body: "Inter" or "DM Sans" — clean, highly readable
- Accent/Labels: "Space Grotesk" — techy, modern
- Minimum body size: 16px. Line-height: 1.7.

**Visual Language:**
- Organic shapes and curved section dividers (think rolling hills, 
  leaf curves) to separate content blocks
- Subtle grain/noise texture overlays on hero and CTA sections for an 
  earthy, tactile feel
- Full-bleed high-resolution photography of African farmers, greenhouse 
  workers, drone farming, produce markets, hands in soil
- Icon style: rounded, filled with a slight illustrated/hand-drawn quality
- Cards with soft shadows, rounded corners (16px radius)
- Micro-interactions: hover lift on cards, underline reveal on nav links, 
  smooth scroll with fade-in animations on sections
- Green gradient overlays on hero images for brand coherence

---

## 📐 LAYOUT PRINCIPLES

- Desktop: 1440px max-width grid, 12 columns, 24px gutter
- Tablet: 768px, 8-column grid
- Mobile: 375px, 4-column grid, all sections fully responsive
- Navigation: Sticky, transparent on hero → solid white with drop shadow 
  on scroll
- Generous white space throughout — this is a premium, trust-building brand
- Sections alternate between light sage/off-white and white backgrounds

## ⚙️ INTERACTION & COMPONENT SPECIFICATIONS

- All buttons: hover state with slight color shift + cursor pointer + 
  subtle scale(1.02)
- Navigation: active state = green underline; hover = gold underline
- Form inputs: focus ring in accent green, floating label animation
- Cards: hover = translateY(-4px) + box-shadow deepens
- Mobile nav: full-screen slide-in overlay with staggered menu item 
  animations
- Course cards: "Enroll" button animates to checkmark on click
- Blog cards: image zoom on hover (scale 1.05, transition 0.3s)
- Accordion FAQ: smooth expand/collapse with rotate chevron icon
- Testimonial carousel: auto-advances every 5s, pause on hover
- Countdown timer: live ticking for webinar
- Newsletter: inline validation, success animation on submit
- Scroll-triggered animations: fade-in-up on section entry (IntersectionObserver)

## 🌿 ASSETS & IMAGERY DIRECTION

All images should reflect:
- African farmers (diverse — male, female, young, older)
- Greenhouse environments, open fields, market stalls
- Modern agri-technology: drones, tablets on farms, irrigation systems
- Close-ups of produce: ginger, leafy greens, grains, vegetables
- Warm golden-hour light and rich green tones throughout
- Stock photo style: authentic, not overly staged

---

## 🏆 OVERALL DESIGN GOAL

This website should feel like the intersection of a premium agri-tech 
startup and a trusted educational institution — think Coursera meets 
Hello Tractor meets Modern Farmer magazine. Clean, professional, deeply 
rooted in the African agricultural context, but globally competitive in 
design quality. Every page should inspire trust, drive enrollment/purchases, 
and make farmers feel seen, valued, and empowered.
